Our Awesome Features

AI Dental Image Analysis

Users can upload images of their teeth, and our system uses AI to detect common dental issues like cavities, gum disease.

Personalized Dental Health Reports

The website generates a detailed report that highlights detected problems, their severity.

Daily Oral Health Tips

The platform offers personalized daily habits and preventive care tips to help users maintain a healthy smile.

Budget-Based clinic Recommendations
Users can input their budget and location to receive suggestions for affordable dental clinics or doctors nearby.
Educational Resources

A dedicated section provides dental care-related articles, and FAQs to educate users about various dental procedures and proper oral hygiene.

Modern Methods of Teeth caring

Invisalign, clear aligners, and lingual braces are popular modern teeth straightening methods.
